About Webi

Webi is an online comparison and research platform for:

  • Physical Goods: Clothing, shoes, electronics, furniture, home decor, books, toys, beauty products, food and beverages
  • Digital Products: E-books, software, online courses, music, videos
  • Services: Consulting, design, writing, marketing
  • Handmade Items: Jewelry, crafts, art, and more

Webi helps users compare prices, features, and deals across online retailers. It also offers tools and tips to make smarter purchase decisions.
